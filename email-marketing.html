What is Email Marketing?

Email marketing is a digital strategy used to communicate with customers directly through emails. It helps promote products, share updates, and build long-term relationships.

Types of Email Marketing

Transactional Emails: Sent automatically after actions like purchases or password resets.

Newsletter Emails: Regular updates about new products and company news.

Promotional Emails: Special offers and discounts to boost sales.
